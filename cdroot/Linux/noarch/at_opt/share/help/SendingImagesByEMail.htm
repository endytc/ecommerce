Sending Images by E-Mail

  1. On the Tools menu press Send by e-mail.
  2. Enter the recipient's e-mail address in the 'To' field (for example, jdoe@company.com).
  3. Enter your e-mail address in the 'From' field (for example, maryoe@company.com).
  4. Enter required text in the 'Subject' field.
  5. Enter the message text (optionally).
  6. Press Save As... . Select the file name and type, under which the image will be saved before sending. They may differ from the name and type of the image you are editing.
  7. Check Delete after sending if you do not want to keep the image on the disk after sending.
  8. Press Send.
