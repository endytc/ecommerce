Changing color depth

  • On the Image menu click Color Bit Depth, and then select the bit depth value.

Bit Depth and the corresponding Image composition values are shown in the table below.

Bit Depth

Image Composition

1 bit

Monochrome (black and white)

8 bit

256 Colors (Color or grayscale)

32 bit

TrueColor
