Using Clipboard

Copying to Clipboard

  • On the menu Edit click Copy.

Pasting over existing image

  1. On the menu Edit click Paste. The border around the image indicates that the image is moveable.
  2. Drag the pasted image to desired position.
  3. Press Enter to fix the pasted image, or press Backspace to cancel the paste operation.

Pasting as new image (Image Manager only)

  1. Go to Image Manager Main Window.
  2. On the menu Edit click Paste New.
